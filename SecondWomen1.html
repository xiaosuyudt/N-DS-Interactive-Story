I went one day alone to a cafe on the Ginza.

I had only ten yen on me.

I said with a smile to the hostess who sat beside me.

“All I’ve got is ten yen. Consider yourself warned.”

“You needn’t worry.”

She spoke with a trace of a Kansai accent.

It was strange how she calmed my agitation with those few words.
